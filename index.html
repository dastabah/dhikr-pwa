Surah Al-Fatihah

(١) بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Bismillāhi-r-Raḥmāni-r-Raḥīm

1. In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful

(٢) الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ

Alḥamdu lillāhi Rabbi-l-ʿālamīn

2. All praise is due to Allah, Lord of the worlds

(٣) الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Ar-Raḥmāni-r-Raḥīm

3. The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful

(٤) مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ

Māliki yawmi-d-dīn

4. Sovereign of the Day of Recompense

(٥) إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

Iyyāka naʿbudu wa iyyāka nastaʿīn

5. It is You we worship and You we ask for help

(٦) اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Ihdinā-ṣ-ṣirāṭa-l-mustaqīm

6. Guide us to the straight path

(٧) صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ

Ṣirāṭa-lladhīna anʿamta ʿalayhim ghayri-l-maghḍūbi ʿalayhim wa la-ḍ-ḍāllīn

7. The path of those upon whom You have bestowed favor, not of those who have earned Your anger or of those who are astray

Sayyidul Istighfar

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي

Allāhumma anta Rabbī

O Allah, You are my Lord

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ

lā ilāha illā Anta

There is no god but You

خَلَقْتَنِي وَأَنَا عَبْدُكَ

khalaqtanī wa anā ʿabduka

You created me and I am Your servant

وَأَنَا عَلَىٰ عَهْدِكَ

wa anā ʿalā ʿahdika

And I am upon Your covenant

وَوَعْدِكَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُ

wa waʿdika mā-staṭaʿtu

and Your promise as much as I can

أَعُوذُ بِكَ

Aʿūdhu bika

I seek refuge in You

مِنْ شَرِّ مَا صَنَعْتُ

min sharri mā ṣanaʿtu

from the evil of what I have done

أَبُوءُ لَكَ

abū'u laka

I acknowledge to you

بِنِعْمَتِكَ عَلَيَّ

bi niʿmatika ʿalayya

Your favor upon me

وَأَبُوءُ بِذَنْبِي

wa abū'u bi dhanbī

and I acknowledge my sin

فَاغْفِرْ لِي

fa-ghfir lī

So forgive me

فَإِنَّهُ لَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ

fa innahu lā yaghfiru-dh-dhunūba

for verily none forgives sins

إِلَّا أَنْتَ

illā Anta

except You

Adhkar

Istighfar - 100 times

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ الْعَظِيمَ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ

Astaghfirullāha-l-ʿAẓīm wa atūbu ilayh

I seek forgiveness from Allah, the Mighty, and I repent to Him

Tahlil - 100 times

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ

Lā ilāha illā Allāh

There is no deity worthy of worship except Allah

Salawat - 100 times

اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَىٰ سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ النُّورِ وَآلِهِ

Allāhumma ṣalli ʿalā Sayyidinā Muḥammadin an-Nūr wa ālih

O Allah, send prayers upon our Master Muhammad ﷺ, the Light, and his Family

The Latifiyyah

يَا لَطِيفًا بِخَلْقِهِ

Yā Laṭīfan bi khalqih

O Gentle One, compassionate with His creation

يَا خَبِيرًا بِخَلْقِهِ

Yā Khabīran bi khalqih

O All-Aware, One who is all-aware of His creation

يَا عَلِيمًا بِخَلْقِهِ

Yā ʿAlīman bi khalqih

O All-Knowing, One who is all-knowledgeable of His Creation

اُلْطُفْ بِنَا وَالْمُسْلِمِينَ

Ulṭuf binā wa-l muslimīn

Be gentle with us and the Muslims

يَا لَطِيفُ، يَا خَبِيرُ، يَا عَلِيمُ

Yā Laṭīfu, Yā Khabīru, Yā ʿAlīmu

O Gentle One, O All-Aware One, O All-Knowing One

Ya Lateefan lam yazal

يَا لَطِيفًا لَمْ يَزَلْ

Yā Laṭīfan lam yazal

O Gentle One who remains forever

اُلْطُفْ بِنَا فِيمَا نَزَلْ

Ulṭuf binā fīmā nazal

Be gentle to us in all that befalls us

أَنْتَ اللَّطِيفُ لَمْ تَزَلْ

Anta-l Laṭīfu lam tazal

You are constantly gentle

اُلْطُفْ بِنَا وَالْمُسْلِمِينَ

Ulṭuf binā wa-l muslimīn

Treat us and the Muslims with gentleness

La ilaha illa Allah

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ، لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ، مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ، عَلَيْهِ وَآلِهِ صَلَّى اللَّهُ

Lā ilāha illā Allāh, Lā ilāha illā Allāh, Muḥammadun Rasūlullāh, ʿalayhi wa ālihi ṣallā Allāh

There is no deity worthy of worship except Allah, There is no deity worthy of worship except Allah, Muhammad ﷺ is the Messenger of Allah, upon him and his family may Allah's blessings be

بِهَا يَثْبُتُ الْإِيمَانُ، بِهَا يَحْصُلُ الْأَمَانُ، كَرِّرْ أَيُّهَا الْإِنْسَانُ: لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ

Bihā yathbutu-l Īmān, bihā yaḥṣulu-l amān, karrir ayyuha-l insān: Lā ilāha illā Allāh

Through it, faith is affirmed. Through it, safety is obtained. Repeat, O human: There is no god but Allah

Al-Qasidatul Muhammadiyyah

مُحَمَّدٌ خَيْرُ مَنْ يَمْشِي عَلَى قَدَمِ

Muhammadun khayru man yamshī 'alā qadami

Muhammad ﷺ is the best of those who walk on two feet

مُحَمَّدٌ أَشْرَفُ الْأَعْرَابِ وَالْعَجَمِ

Muhammadun ashraful a'rābi wal 'ajami

Muhammad ﷺ is the noblest of Arabs and non-Arabs

Ma Lana Mawlan Siwa Allah

اللَّهُ اللَّهُ اللَّهُ اللَّهُ مَا لَنَا مَوْلًى سِوَى اللَّهِ

Allāhu Allāhu Allāhu Allāh, mā lanā mawlan siwā Allāh

Allah, Allah, Allah, Allah, we have no protector besides Allah

كُلَّمَا نَادَيْتُ يَا هُوَ | جَاءَنِي اللَّطْفُ مِنَ اللَّهِ

Kullamā nādaytu yā Hū, jā'anī al-luṭfu min Allāh

Whenever I would call upon 'The One,' His gentle grace would envelop me

أَقْبَلَ السَّاقِي عَلَيْنَا قَدَّمَ الْكَأْسَ إِلَيْنَا

Aqbalas sāqī 'alaynā, qaddamal ka'sa ilaynā

The cupbearer approached us, he presented the cup to us

فَاحْتَسَيْنَا وَارْتَوَيْنَا مِنْ كُؤُوسِ الْهَاشِمِيَّة

Faḥtasaynā wartawaynā, min ku'ūsi-l Hāshimiyyah

We drank and were quenched from the Hashimi cups
